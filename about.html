Hi we are a group of people brought together by coffee, arunikacoffe.com was created out of love we like international quality mandailing and sipirok coffee. We admire coffee in everything the shape; from coffee trees to cherries, to green beans, and finally from roasted beans to a cup.

In our own region of North Sumatra in Indonesia, we work with farmers to produces high quality arabica coffee beans, which we believe to be one of the best coffees in world.

We spent time with the local coffee farming community and were involved from start to finish mid to post harvest. We base our work on trust, integrity and taste respect. At arunikacoffe.com, we are confident to give you the peace of mind that our coffee will meet the specifications you need.

Arunika coffee was founded in November 2020 to be able to sell in the local market and exporting better quality coffee beans from our own plantations and our partner farmers. Business we cover green coffee bean processing, wholesale, and roaster work orders for the international market and domestic.
